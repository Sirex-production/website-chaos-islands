Welcome, Highlander

City-building meets deck-building in Chaos Islands. Defend against demons, explore the skies!"

"Chaos Islands" combines the tranquility of town building, the strategic depth of card games, and the enchanting allure of medieval fantasy.

With its rich lore, struggle between light and darkness, players are invited to create their own haven in the skies, and work towards restoring balance to a world torn asunder by demonic forces.
